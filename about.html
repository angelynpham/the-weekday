Known professionally by his stage name, The Weeknd, Abel Makkonen Tesfaye is both a Canadian singer and songwriter. Abel co-founded his record label, XO, in 2011 and released his first set of mixtapes, House of Balloons, Thursday, and Echoes of Silence. The critically acclaimed artist has five studio albums, with his most recent being ‘Dawn FM’, which features legendary music producer, Quincy Jones and was released in 2022.
